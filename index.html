About Old Rusty

Old Rusty Transport and Recovery delivers dependable transport and roadside support across the South East, based in East Grinstead. We treat every vehicle like it’s our own—whether it’s a daily driver or a track toy. Fully insured for transport, recovery, and roadside support—including goods in transit, public liability, and road risks—so your vehicle is protected every step of the way

Our Services

  • Roadside recovery
  • Classic & vintage vehicle transport
  • Auction pickups
  • Track day support
  • Temporary small puncture repairs to get you to a tyre fitter
  • Out of fuel assistance
